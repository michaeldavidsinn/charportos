Char’s Recipe is a food brand specializing in Mentai Rice, offering a rich and creamy dining experience with premium ingredients and customizable toppings. With a stylish and modern branding approach, Char’s Recipe aims to capture the hearts of food lovers who crave delicious, indulgent, and visually appealing dishes.

The design supports this with a visually appealing and modern layout featuring a soft pastel palette in pink and beige for a warm, inviting feel. High-quality food photography prominently displays the creamy Mentai Rice and its toppings, while clean typography and subtle graphic elements add a contemporary touch, effectively communicating the brand's focus on delicious and visually attractive food.
