iAware

Role

AI Seminar

Project

Experience Design 2024

Technologies

Canva

iAware is a branding and event project focused on making Artificial Intelligence more accessible and understandable for older generations. The theme, "How AI Distorts Reality", explores the influence of AI in everyday life through an engaging talk session. The design blends educational purpose with visual excitement, aiming to bridge generations through tech literacy and creative outreach.

The design embodies this mission by blending modern, tech-inspired visuals with elements that resonate with older generations, creating an approachable aesthetic. Thought-provoking graphics illustrating AI's influence aim to demystify the technology, while the overall design strives to be both informative and exciting, fostering tech literacy and intergenerational understanding around the "How AI Distorts Reality" theme.
